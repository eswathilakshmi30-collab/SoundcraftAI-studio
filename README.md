SoundCraftAI – AI Powered Music Composition Agent

SoundCraftAI is an AI-powered music generation platform that creates original, royalty-free music compositions for multimedia projects such as films, games, YouTube videos, podcasts, and advertisements. The system analyzes mood, genre, tempo, and duration requirements to automatically generate adaptive music with seamless looping and stylistic arrangements.

The platform leverages generative AI to compose structured music pieces including chord progressions, melodies, and rhythmic patterns, which are converted into playable audio outputs.

Features
🎵 AI-generated royalty-free music compositions
🎧 Support for multiple genres (LoFi, Cinematic, Ambient, EDM, Classical, Rock)
😊 Mood-based music generation (Happy, Sad, Epic, Relaxing, Suspense)
⏱ Custom duration control for multimedia projects
🔁 Seamless looping for background music
🎼 Automatic tempo and chord progression generation
📥 Downloadable audio output
🧠 Powered by Generative AI agents
Use Cases
Background music for YouTube videos
Game soundtracks
Podcast intro/outro music
Short film scoring
Advertising and multimedia production
Tech Stack
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Backend: Python (FastAPI)
AI Model: Generative AI (Gemini)
Music Processing: music21, pretty_midi, pydub
How It Works
User selects genre, mood, tempo, and duration.
The AI agent analyzes the parameters.
The system generates chord progressions, melody patterns, and arrangement structure.
Music is converted into MIDI and WAV audio format.
The generated music can be previewed and downloaded.
Goal

SoundCraftAI aims to simplify music creation by enabling creators and developers to instantly generate custom background music without licensing issues.
